Privacy Policy for Taskila

Data Collection & Storage: 
Taskila is designed with a local-first architecture. All tasks, notes, attachments, and data you create are stored securely on your device's internal storage using a local database. We do not operate external servers, and we do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data or entries.

Permissions & App Features:
Taskila requests specific Android permissions to provide core functionality knowingly initiated by the user:
• Notifications: Used solely to deliver your task reminders.
• Storage / File Access: Used strictly to allow you to select, view, and attach photos or files directly to your tasks. These files remain stored locally on your device.

Google Drive Backup & Sync (Pro Feature):
If you choose to use the optional Google Drive Sync feature, Taskila will request authorization to access your Google Drive using the secure "drive.appdata" scope. 
• This permission allows Taskila to see, create, and delete ONLY its own configuration and backup files inside an isolated, hidden folder dedicated to Taskila.
• Taskila cannot view, access, edit, or delete any other files, photos, or documents in your personal Google Drive account.
• Your backup data is transmitted directly from your device to your personal Google Drive storage. We do not collect, intercept, or share your Google account information or backup data.

Third-Party Access:
We do not use third-party analytics, tracking networks, or advertising SDKs. Your data belongs to you.
